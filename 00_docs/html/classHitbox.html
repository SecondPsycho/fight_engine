◆ collidesDir()

int* Hitbox::collidesDir ( Hitbox  rect)
inline

Gives a collision detection that includes what side the collision is happening on.

Parameters
rectThe other hitbox to check collision against.
Returns
int* An array containing directional collision data. The first indicates x collision (negative means collision on the left, positive is on the right). The second indicates y collision (negative means collision on the bottom, positive is on the top). If either of the previous two are -2 or 2, the system thinks that collision should be applied first. The third integer indicates 1 for collision and 0 for no collision.
